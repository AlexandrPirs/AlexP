Frequently Asked Questions

Your question’s not answered here? [Ask in our community]

How much does it cost?

Actor OpenSource edition and Cloud solutions will be always free. We believe that communications can't have any limits. Actor Enterprise edition provide Enterprise features for money. You always can ask us to implement something that is missing.

What about encryption?

We use 256-bit AES encryption for transmitting App to Server. In Enterprise edition it is available full end-to-end encryption.

Why are you using SMS authentication?

SMS authentication is safest available authentication method. We don't use any kind of passwords because they are almost always weak. We have email and OAuth2 authentication support also.
